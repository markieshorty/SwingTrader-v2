Find optimum weights

The optimizer tries ~30 dial variations around your current production settings against the historic dataset — small nudges, bigger single-signal shifts, and structurally different mixes (equal weights, technical-heavy, sentiment-heavy). To guard against flukes, it tunes on the earlier ~70% of the window, then re-tests the best configuration on the held-out remainder it never saw. A config that only wins on the data it was tuned on is noise — the result tells you plainly whether the winner held up. Candidates are judged on market-adjusted expectancy (each trade's return minus SPY's move over the same days), with guardrails: at least {{ 40 }} trades, and a max drawdown no worse than 1.25× the baseline's.

Runs ~30 full simulations server-side — expect 15–25 minutes.
