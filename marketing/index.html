The routine, automated

A handful of agents run on a schedule so the reading, sorting and summarising happen without you.

  1. 1

    Curate

    Sifts a broad universe of listed companies each week into a focused watchlist worth paying attention to.

  2. 2

    Research

    Reviews each name daily — price behaviour, news flow, filings — and turns it into structured notes.

  3. 3

    Brief

    Sends a plain-English morning email summarising what changed and what might deserve a closer look.

  4. 4

    Organise

    Keeps everything you follow in one dashboard — lists, notes, history and activity in a single place.

  5. 5

    Review

    An analytics lab for looking backwards — explore how ideas and rules would have played out historically.

Questions

What is Cadentic, exactly?

Personal software that automates the routine of following markets — curating watchlists, gathering research, and summarising it on a schedule — for a small group of invited users.

Is this financial advice?

No. Cadentic does not provide investment advice or recommendations, and nothing on this site is an invitation to engage in investment activity. Investing carries risk, including loss of capital.

Can I sign up?

Not publicly — Cadentic is a private, invite-only project and there is no open registration or paid service.

Does Cadentic hold my money?

No. Cadentic never holds funds and never has access to money. Users connect their own third-party accounts with keys they control and can revoke at any time.
