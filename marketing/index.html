A full trading desk, automated

Five steps run on a schedule, every trading day. You approve; it executes.

  1. 1

    Screen

    Scans a universe of liquid stocks each week and builds a focused watchlist.

  2. 2

    Research

    Scores every candidate on momentum, volume, relative strength, price levels and fundamentals.

  3. 3

    Decide

    Ranks the day's setups and emails you a brief. You approve the trades — or let it run hands-free.

  4. 4

    Execute

    Places sized orders through your Trading 212 account, within your capital and position limits.

  5. 5

    Manage

    Watches every open position and handles stop-loss, targets, trailing stops and time exits for you.

Questions

Do you hold my money?

No. Acme Trading connects to your own Trading 212 account via API keys you control. Trades happen in your account; you can revoke access at any time.

Is this financial advice?

No. Acme Trading is an automation tool that executes rules you configure. It doesn't give advice, and past performance doesn't predict future results. Trading carries risk of loss.

Can I try it without risking real money?

Yes — start in demo mode against Trading 212's practice environment. Nothing goes live until you explicitly switch to live mode.

What if the market drops hard?

A daily-loss circuit breaker auto-pauses new entries, and you can pause manually any time. Your open positions keep their stop-losses and targets throughout.
